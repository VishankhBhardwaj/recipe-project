lasagna

Ingridients

  • Meat sauce.
  • Noodles.
  • Ricotta mixture.
  • Mozzarella slices.
  • Meat sauce.
  • Parmesan cheese.

Recipe

  1. Brown 1 lb ground beef with onions and garlic. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, sugar, and seasonings (basil, oregano, salt, pepper). Simmer for 20-30 minutes.

  2. Mix ricotta cheese, an egg, Parmesan, parsley (optional), salt, and pepper in a bowl.

  3. In a baking dish, layer cooked (or no-boil) lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with cheese on top.

  4. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 20-25 minutes until cheese is golden. Let rest for 10-15 minutes before serving.

Rasmalai

Ingridients

  • Paneer
  • Sweetened Milk

Recipe

  1. Boil 1 liter of milk, add lemon juice or vinegar to curdle it. Drain and press the curds (chenna) to remove excess water. Knead the chenna into a smooth dough and shape it into small balls.

  2. Boil 2 cups of water with 1 cup sugar to make a syrup. Add the chenna balls and cook for 10-12 minutes until they expand. Remove and cool.

  3. Simmer 1 liter of milk until reduced to half. Add 1/4 cup sugar, cardamom, and saffron, and cook for another 10 minutes.

  4. Squeeze excess syrup from the chenna balls and add them to the rabri. Garnish with pistachios, almonds, and a few saffron strands. Chill before serving.
